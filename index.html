About the Employer

Guelph, a vibrant community in southern Ontario with over 120,000 residents, holds a rich history dating back to its founding in 1827. Interestingly, the land on which Guelph stands was utilized by Native Americans over 11,000 years ago. Today, the city is celebrated for its picturesque and diverse natural beauty, featuring well-maintained trails that allow residents and visitors to explore its lush surroundings. Guelph takes pride in its commitment to environmental sustainability, demonstrated through ongoing efforts to preserve green spaces, enhancing the city's overall ecological richness.

I'm part of the IT Service Desk team at City Hall, where I collaborate with a diverse group of individuals responsible for maintaining various aspects of the city. From building planners to the police and fire department, as well as other infrastructure services, our team plays a crucial role in supporting the people who ensure the smooth operation of the city.

My Role

In my role as an IT Service Desk Support professional at the City of Guelph, I acted as the initial point of contact for users facing technical challenges. Responsibilities involved overseeing the functionality of IT hardware and software systems, identifying and resolving technical issues, and ensuring overall system efficiency.

Throughout the term I provided IT support to users via phone, email, and walk-ins, addressing hardware, software, and network issues. This included guiding users through troubleshooting steps, maintaining detailed records, and resolving technical issues. Collaborating with the IT team, I contributed to resolving complex issues, monitoring system alerts, and supporting ongoing projects. My commitment to delivering quality service played a vital role in ensuring the smooth operation of the City of Guelph's technology infrastructure.

Goals

  1. Communication - Learn to better communicate both issues and solutions.

    Successfully achieving my goal to better my communication skills involved actively managing the helpdesk call line, where I engaged with users to understand and communicate solutions more effectively. Through regular self-reflection, feedback from colleagues and users, and an adaptive approach to communication strategies, I made noticeable improvements. These refined skills not only boosted my professional capabilities but also positively impacted user satisfaction and the overall effectiveness of managing the IT Helpdesk call line.

  2. Technical - Learn and understand the IT systems used by corporations.

    Achieving a profound understanding of corporate IT systems was realized through hands-on engagement in the IT Helpdesk. This practical experience allowed for in-depth learning about various systems and their interdependencies. Success was measured by the ability to troubleshoot issues effectively—identifying specific system problems and implementing solutions. This accomplishment showcases the significance of hands-on experience in comprehending IT systems and troubleshooting processes.

  3. Collaborative - Strengthen my collaborative and teamwork skills.

    Successfully improving collaborative skills involved actively seeking help, fostering coworker relationships, and enhancing teamwork. This facilitated seamless collaboration on user concerns and addressed complex issues, emphasizing the importance of interpersonal skills in a team-oriented environment.

Achievements

Resolved a critical Chrome script issue affecting the Cisco Finesse software, which was causing abrupt user disconnects for hundreds of city employees.

Spearheaded the discovery and implementation of a key AutoCAD licensing script, resolving licensing-related issues that impacted a significant number of city employees.

Edited and maintained the SharePoint website, ensuring up-to-date content and seamless functionality for users. Collaborated with teams to implement necessary changes and updates
